# Subscribe

To subscribe, you must meet several requirements first.

:::info
Subscription requirements:
- Already have a SSO Cendekia account
- Already [login](../autentikasi/masuk.md) to your account
- It's complete [school data](./data-sekolah.md)
:::

## Subscription Steps

1. After logging in to Gradynex, select the **Subscription** menu
2. Click the **New Subscription** button
3. Choose a product that suits your school's needs
4. Select subscription duration (monthly, yearly, etc.)
5. Enter the desired subdomain name for your school website
6. (Optional) Enter the promo code if any, then click **Apply**
7. Click the **Proceed to Payment** button
8. Select an available payment method
9. Make payment according to instructions
10. Once payment is received, your subscription will automatically activate

## Notes

- Subscription will become active after successful payment
- You can change your subscription plan at any time
- Technical support is available 24/7 for our customers
