# Trial

Gradynex offers a 14-day free trial package for new users who want to try our service.

:::info
To use our trial, you must:
- Already have a SSO Cendekia account
- Already [login](../autentikasi/masuk.md) to your account
- It's complete [school data](./data-sekolah.md)
:::

## Steps to Activate Trial

1. After logging in to Gradynex, select the **Subscription** menu
2. If you've never subscribed, you'll see a 14-day free trial package option
3. Click the **Try it Free** button
4. Fill in the form provided with the required data
5. Click the submit button
6. Your trial will automatically be active for 14 days

## Notes

- Trial is valid for 14 days
- After the trial period ends, you can continue [subscribe](./berlangganan.md) to continue using the service
- All features are available during the trial period
