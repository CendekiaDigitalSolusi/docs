# How to Enter (Login)

## Login with Email and Password

1. Open [gradynex.com/login](https://gradynex.com/login).
2. Click the **Sign in with SSO** button or you will be automatically directed to the SSO login page.
3. Fill in your email and password.
4. Click Sign in.
5. If successful, you will return to the original application.

:::info
Gradynex accounts use the Scholar SSO (Single Sign-On) system.

- If you don't have an account, register on the page [SSO Cendekia - Register](https://accounts.cendekiadigitalsolusi.co.id/register).
- If you forget your password, use the guide [Forgot Password](./lupa-kata-sandi.md).
:::

## Other Authentication Methods

In addition to email and password, you can also log in using supported social accounts on the Scholar SSO page:

- Google
- Microsoft

The complete guide is available at [Login with Google or Microsoft](/sso-cendekia/pengguna/login-sosial).

## If the Access Approval Page Appears

1. Check the name of the application requesting access.
2. Check the requested data (e.g. email and basic profile).
3. Click Allow to continue.

## If Login Failed?

- Make sure the email and password are correct.
- Check whether keyboard Caps Lock is active.
- Use the **Forgot Password** menu if you forget your password.
- If the account is locked, wait a few minutes and then try again.
