# Forgot Password

If you forget the SSO Cendekia account password used to log in to Gradynex, use the reset process below.

## Password Reset Steps

1. Go to the SSO Cendekia login page via Gradynex at [gradynex.com/login](https://gradynex.com/login).
2. Click the **Forgot Password** button.
3. Enter your SSO Cendekia account email.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Open the reset email from SSO Cendekia (check the Spam folder if it is not there).
6. Click the reset link from the SSO Cendekia email.
7. Enter a new password and confirm the SSO Cendekia account password.
8. Click **Change Password**.
9. Login again with the new password.

You can also go directly to the SSO Cendekia reset page at [accounts.cendekiadigital Solusi.co.id/forgot-password](https://accounts.cendekiadigitalsolusi.co.id/forgot-password).

## Vital Records

- The SSO Cendekia reset link is only valid temporarily (usually 24 hours).
- If the link expires, repeat the process from the beginning.
- After reset, your login session on the other device may end.
- If the email is not received, check your Spam folder or contact support.
