# How to Enter (Login)

To access the Gradynex tenant application, you need to have a registered account. If you don't have an account, contact your school administrator.

## Login Steps

1. Go to the login page on your school domain (example:`namasekolah.sch.id/login`).
2. Enter your username and password.
3. Click the **Sign In** button.
4. If the data is correct, you will be logged in [dashboards](../user-panel/dashboard.md).

:::info
**For new administrators:** Your username and password will be sent to the email registered in our system when the service is activated for the first time.
:::

## If Login Failed?

- Make sure the username and password are correct.
- Check whether keyboard Caps Lock is active.
- Use the **Forgot Password** menu if you forget your password.
- Contact your school administrator for further assistance.
