# Forgot Password

If you forget your Gradynex tenant account password, use the following reset process.

## Password Reset Steps

1. Go to the login page on your school domain (example:`namasekolah.sch.id/login`).
2. Click the **Forgot Password** link.
3. Enter your account email.
4. Click the **Send Reset Link** button.
5. Open the email (check the Spam folder if it's not there).
6. Click the reset link from the Gradynex email.
7. Enter a new password and confirm the password.
8. Click **Change Password**.
9. Login again with the new password.

## Vital Records

- The reset link is only valid temporarily.
- If the link expires, repeat the process from the beginning.
- After reset, login sessions on other devices may end.
- If the email is not received, check your Spam folder or contact your administrator.
