# News

News is a feature for writing and publishing dynamic content to public pages of school websites.

This feature is suitable for:
- School announcements
- Activity coverage
- Educational articles
- PPDB info
- Other information is dynamic

## Fast Flow: From Blank to Live

### 1. Create a Draft
1. Click the **Add New** button
2. Fill in the headline
3. Enter the content/news content
4. Click **Save As Draft**

### 2. Tidy up your news identity
1. Set news **Category**
2. Add relevant **Tags**
3. Upload **Featured Image** (featured image)
4. Set URL **Slug** (usually automatically from title)
5. Add **Short Description** (excerpt)

### 3. Publish
1. If you are ready to appear publicly, click the **Publish** button
2. Your news is now accessible to the public

## News Page Elements

### News List
On the **News** main page, you see a table listing the articles that have been created.

**Toolbar above the table:**
- **Search**: Quick filter by news headlines
- **Action Dropdown**: Bulk actions for selected articles
- **Column Dropdown**: Choose which columns are displayed
- **Number of rows dropdown**: Set how much data per page

**Table column:**
- **Title** - Headline of the story
- **Category** - The category in which the news is published
- **Writer** - The person who creates the news
- **Date** - When the news was published
- **Status** - Draft or Published
- **Action** - Edit/Delete button

## Tips

- Create an interesting and descriptive title
- Use categories to organize content
- Add images to make the content more interesting
- Use tags to make it easier to find related content
