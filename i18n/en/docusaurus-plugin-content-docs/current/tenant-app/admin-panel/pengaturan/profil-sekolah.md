# School Profile

The **School Profile** page stores school identity data that is used in various places in the system.

This data usually appears in:
- Public website
- Official profile page
- Website footer
- Metadata and integration

:::warning
**Important:** Fill in this data correctly from the start. Some data (such as NPSN and addresses) are often used for administration and integration with other systems.
:::

## How to Access

1. Entered into [Admin Dashboard](../dashboard-admin.md)
2. Open the **Admin menu → Settings → School Profile**

## Recommended Charging Flow

If this is your first time setting up, follow this sequence to avoid confusing address dropdowns:

### 1. Fill in Basic Identity
- **School Name**: The official name of your school
- **NPSN**: National School Principal Number

### 2. Fill in the address step by step
Choose in stages:
1. **Province**
2. **City/Regency**
3. **Subdistrict**
4. **Subdistrict/Village**
5. **Complete Address**: Fill in the detailed street address/no. House

### 3. Complete the Public Profile
- **Principal Name**: Name of the school leader
- **Welcome Message**: Greeting from the principal (optional)
- **School Description**: A short description or motto of the school
- **Profile URL**: Link to the official profile/school website (optional)

### 4. Fill in Statistics & Social Media
- **Number of Teachers**: Total teachers in the school
- **Number of Students**: Total students in the school
- **Number of Staff**: Total staff/employees
- **Number of Alumni**: Total alumni (optional)
- **Social Media**: Links to official accounts (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc)

### 5. Save
- Scroll down the page
- Click the **Save** button to save the changes

## Tips

- **Accurate data** - Make sure all data matches the school's official data
- **Quality photos** - Use high-quality photos of the principal and school visuals
- **Periodic updates** - Updates on the number of students, teachers and other information every new school year
- **Active social media** - Make sure social media links lead to official and active accounts
- **NPSN valid** - Verify your NPSN on the Ministry of Education and Culture website before filling in
